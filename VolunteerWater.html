As a Water Search and Rescue volunteer, you will play a crucial role in responding to emergency situations involving water-based incidents. Whether it's a missing person, a boating accident, or a natural disaster, your skills, and expertise will be essential in locating and rescuing individuals in distress.

Responsibilities:

Participate in regular training sessions to maintain and enhance Water Search and Rescue skills. Conduct water-based searches using specialized equipment and techniques.
Assist in the rescue and recovery of individuals involved in water-related incidents.
Collaborate with other emergency response teams and agencies during joint operations.
Provide support and assistance to survivors and their families during difficult times.
Promote water safety awareness through community outreach programs and educational initiatives.

Requirements:
• Strong swimming skills and comfort in aquatic environments.
• Proficiency in water search and rescue techniques, including swift water rescue, diving, and boat handling.
• Certification in CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation) and First Aid is highly desirable.
• Physical fitness and the ability to perform strenuous activities in challenging environments.
• Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
Availability to respond to emergency calls on a flexible basis.
